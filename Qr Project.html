QR Based Restaurant System

I created a responsive restaurant management app using Django as the backend framework. The app features a QR-based menu with auto table recognition, allowing customers to order instantly by scanning the QR code at their table, eliminating the need to wait for a waiter. It includes separate admin privileges, enabling only the admin to edit the menu, add new items, update or delete existing items, and check all orders from different tables. Customers can view the entire menu after scanning the QR code, add items to their order by clicking "add to cart," and confirm their order once done. They can also delete or add items as needed and check the grand total. This user-friendly ordering experience minimizes hassle and saves a lot of time, running locally within the restaurant's range.
