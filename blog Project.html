Blogs By Bash

I designed and developed a creative blog website to learn how to manipulate data in Django. The website is fully responsive, making it accessible from different screen resolutions. It includes user account management features such as account creation, authentication, and deletion. Additionally, it offers blog management capabilities, allowing for the creation, deletion, and editing of blog posts. The comments management system ensures relevant comments are displayed for each post, with an option to delete comments as needed.
